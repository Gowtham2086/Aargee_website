About Aargee Equipment Pvt. Ltd.

Engineering Excellence Since 1979

Aargee Equipment Pvt. Ltd. has been at the forefront of transportation technology for over four decades. As an ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949:2016 certified company, we specialize in the design, development, and manufacturing of advanced electronic solutions for the mobility sector.

Our comprehensive portfolio includes state-of-the-art Passenger Information Systems (PIS), Driver Information Systems, TFT displays, and custom embedded electronics. Trusted by leading OEMs and State Transport Undertakings across India, we are committed to delivering reliable, innovative, and high-quality products that enhance public transportation infrastructure.
