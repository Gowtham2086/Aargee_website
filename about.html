Our Heritage

Four Decades of
Engineering Leadership

Founded in 1979, Aargee Equipment Pvt. Ltd. is a pioneering force in India's transportation electronics industry. For over four decades, we have been at the forefront of designing and manufacturing advanced vehicle technologies, intelligent mobility solutions, and safety-critical systems that power the nation's transport infrastructure.

Holding both ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949:2016 certifications, our engineering and manufacturing capabilities are benchmarked against the highest global standards. We serve OEMs, State Transport Undertakings, bus body builders, and system integrators across India and international markets.

Our portfolio spans automotive lighting systems, ITMS, Public transit display system, TFT display solutions, and advanced safety technologies — each engineered to the highest standards of reliability and performance.
