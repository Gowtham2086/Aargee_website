OUR STORY

Engineering at the heart
of every journey.

Founded in 1979, Aargee Equipment Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949:2016 certified engineering and manufacturing company specialising in transportation electronics, intelligent transport management systems, passenger information systems, and advanced mobility technologies.

For over four decades, we have been the trusted technology partner of OEMs, State Transport Undertakings, bus body builders, and system integrators across India — delivering products that are synonymous with reliability, quality, and innovation.
